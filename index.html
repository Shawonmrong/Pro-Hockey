Clients Question

You can also browse the topics below to find what you are looking for.

Are refreshments provided?

Yes, lunch and refreshments are provided at our events.

Do I need to be a hockey player to become a Hockey Maker?

No - great volunteers can come from any background. All we ask of Hockey Makers is a bucket load of enthusiasm, a positive can-do attitude as well as a willingness to work as team and show initiative. There are certain roles within the team which require a certain level of experience but we definitely have roles for everyone!

Can I share volunteering opportunities at my club with Hockey Makers?

Absolutely, we would love to share your opportunities with the Hockey Maker database. Click here and head over to fill in the form details and you will become a provider on our system. You can then upload any opportunities. If someone from your club/association/organisation has created a provider account before, please liaise with them and input the opportunity on this existing provider account.

Are accessible roles available?

Yes, we have a wide range of roles available and a bit of something for everyone! If you are unsure which would be the best role for you, please email: pro-hockey@info.com and we can help you find the perfect role. We are also very happy to tailor different roles if needed.

Do I get a choice about what role I do?

Across our smaller events, everyone will get involved with a bit of everything so there aren’t assigned roles prior to the event. For larger events (e.g. International Fixtures, The Super 6s) you do have a choice in which roles you would like to get involved in. Some roles do require a certain level of experience but we think there is a bit of something for everyone!
